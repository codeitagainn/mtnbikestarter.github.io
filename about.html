Start Mountain Biking

About

Mountain biking is an amazing sport that will take you to some great places in your life, but its not the most straightforward to get started in.
The terminology, and technical details can be overwhelming for a beginner and it doesn’t help when different terms are used to describe the same thing.
Our goal with Start Mountain Biking is to help people go from “I’m interested in mountain biking” to “This is one of the best decisions I’ve ever made” in a shorter time.
We do this by breaking down terms you should know, helping you select the right bike, and bringing you the best biking resources on the web.
Start Mountain Biking makes money by receiving a commissions from Amazon for bikes and gear bought through the links on our site. All other links are non-affiliate.
We have also made a commitment to donating 25% of proceeds to the IMBA (international mountain biking association). You can read more about our commitment here.
