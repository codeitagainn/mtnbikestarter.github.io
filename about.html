Start Mountain Biking

About

Mountain biking will be responsible for some of the best times of your life, but its not the most straightforward sport to get started in.
The terminology and technical details can be overwhelming for anyone just beginning. Start Mountain Biking breaks down only what you need to get started and then points you in the right direction for continued growth.
One of our goals with Start Mountain Biking is to help more people go from “I’m interested in mountain biking” to “This is one of the best decisions I’ve ever made”.
We do this by breaking down terms you should know, helping you select the right bike, and connecting you with other biking resources and communities on the web.
The Start Mountain Biking logo was created by Arthur Shlain and discovered on The Noun Project.
