A good mountain bike for you would be the:


Diamondback Catch 2 - Medium
You’re looking to go all in on Mountain Biking right away, and we admire that. A good bike for you would be the Catch 2 by Diamondback. With 27.5” inch wheels you will get a great mix of control, speed, and comfort. The Catch 2 is a full suspension bike with 130mm of front travel to cushion terrain and drops. This bike also comes with a dropper post, so you can easily change your seat height mid ride based on your needs in the moment, put the seat higher when you want to ride fast on some flat paths, and lower when causing over rocks. This bike comes 95% assembled. If you are uncomfortable with the remaining 5% your local bike shop will be able to handle it for $20-30, but be sure to ask.
