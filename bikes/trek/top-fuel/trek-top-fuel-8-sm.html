Trek Top Fuel 8 - Small
You are looking to try different styles of mountain biking for somewhere between $500 and $1000. With 29" tires, a strong frame, and 100mm of suspension travel, you will be able to take on any beginner - intermediate trail at a good speed. The company also provides assembly services for free at your local bike shop if assembly is something you are uncomfortable with.
In the not so distant past it wouldn't be recommended to enter the sport with bikes priced under $1000 due to quality issues, but with rapid advances in technology quality frames and components can be found on bikes a much lower price, much like this 29" Diamondback Overdrive Hardtail.
