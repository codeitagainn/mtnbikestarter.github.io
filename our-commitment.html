Social Responsibility

The passion for riding, adventure, and camaraderie shared by bikers everywhere is inspiring and an all around good time. There is a lot of time and effort put into making this community as special as it is. The people behind that time and effort are what make our craft possible.
Start Mountain Biking is invested in seeing the community continue to grow and thrive by donating 25% of all revenue to the International Mountain Biking Association (IMBA) for life.
“The IMBA is the worldwide leader in mountain bike advocacy, and the only organization in the United States focused entirely on trails, access, education and experiences for all types of mountain bikers in all parts of the country”
The work they do enables us to ride clean trails, better understand our sport, and meet other awesome members of the biking community, and keeps us safe. We are excited to partner with this amazing organization.
If you’d like to get involved with the IMBA, and we encourage you to do so, you can find out how on their website
