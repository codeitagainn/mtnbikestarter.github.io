Social Responsibility

Our service is only as good as our community and our community is fantastic.
The passion for riding, adventure, and camaraderie shared by bikers everywhere is inspiring and are invested in seeing it continue to grow and thrive.
Start Mountain Biking is donates 25% of all revenue to the International Mountain Biking Association (IMBA) for life.
“The IMBA is the worldwide leader in mountain bike advocacy, and the only organization in the United States focused entirely on trails, access, education and experiences for all types of mountain bikers in all parts of the country”
The work they do brings the community together, helps us it grow, and keeps us safe.
If you’d like to get involved with the IMBA you can find out how on their website
