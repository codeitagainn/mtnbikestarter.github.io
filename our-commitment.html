Social Responsibility

We believe that if you have the opportunity to make a difference you should.
That is why Start Mountain Biking is donates 25% of all proceeds to the IMBA.
“The International Mountain Bicycling Association (IMBA) is the worldwide leader in mountain bike advocacy, and the only organization in the United States focused entirely on trails, access, education and experiences for all types of mountain bikers in all parts of the country”
You can read more about the IMBA on their website
We believe strongly in the work they’re doing to foster growth of the mountain bike community.
We hope to provide increased exposure to the work the important work the IMBA does and encourage you to get involved.
