About Mountain Biking Terms

  • add_circleSingle Track
    Narrow trail designed for one rider at a time.
  • add_circleTrailhead
    Entrance to a trail system. Typically near where you park your car and get setup.
  • add_circleCorners
    Turns on a bike trail
  • add_circleDouble
    Jump with a gap in the middle that needs to be cleared
  • add_circleTabletop
    Jump with a filled in gap. These are less dangerous than doubles

Bike Terms

  • add_circleSuspension
    Spring and shock absorbers that cushion your bike from rough terrain
  • add_circleFrame
    Main component of a bicycle, onto which wheels and other components are fitted.
  • add_circleHardtail
    Mountain bike with front suspension.
  • add_circleFull Suspension
    Mountain bike with front and rear suspension.
  • add_circleRigid
    Mountain bike with no suspension.
  • add_circleFull Suspension
    Mountain bike with front and rear suspension..
  • add_circleSadle
    Your bike seat
  • add_circleDisc Brakes
    Brakes that use the friction of pads against a disc that is attached to the wheel. Any mountain bike should use disc brakes and not rim brakes.
  • add_circleRim Brakes
    Brakes that use friction of pads against the tire rim. Not freqently seen on mountain bikes.
